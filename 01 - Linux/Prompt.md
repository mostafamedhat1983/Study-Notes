**mosta@Mostafa-LT:~$**

- `mosta` = your current **username**.
    
- `Mostafa-LT` = your machine’s hostname, meaning the computer you are logged into.
    
- `~` = your home directory, a shortcut for something like `/home/mosta`.
    
- `$` = you are logged in as a regular user, not root
-  # you are logged in as root
