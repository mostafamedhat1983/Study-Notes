Zettelkasten is ==a personal knowledge management system that uses a network of linked, atomic notes to help users capture, connect, and generate ideas==. Developed by sociologist [Niklas Luhmann](https://www.google.com/search?sca_esv=d8b03bc1a9f4816e&sxsrf=AE3TifNc-_XmeRczFBdxs5GgKVdOt4DxdA%3A1764255327734&q=Niklas+Luhmann&source=lnms&fbs=AIIjpHxU7SXXniUZfeShr2fp4giZ1Y6MJ25_tmWITc7uy4KIeoJTKjrFjVxydQWqI2NcOhYPURIv2wPgv_w_sE_0Sc6QqqU7k8cSQndc5mTXCIWHa_uc-TjDJYRtLl-RKXlVOTL5mI-WiiglTJRFGvAEXXnfWyfSz0RcKTdc0SVijutDrGKDjzC7BDwXiykPpSqg3pwIolHdg3emZvghVVSMs5T2veRtSA&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwjjvo-ky5KRAxXhAvsDHSaKBlEQgK4QegYIAQgAEAY&biw=1912&bih=924&dpr=1), the method involves creating short, self-contained notes on a single idea ([zettels](https://www.google.com/search?sca_esv=d8b03bc1a9f4816e&sxsrf=AE3TifNc-_XmeRczFBdxs5GgKVdOt4DxdA%3A1764255327734&q=zettels&source=lnms&fbs=AIIjpHxU7SXXniUZfeShr2fp4giZ1Y6MJ25_tmWITc7uy4KIeoJTKjrFjVxydQWqI2NcOhYPURIv2wPgv_w_sE_0Sc6QqqU7k8cSQndc5mTXCIWHa_uc-TjDJYRtLl-RKXlVOTL5mI-WiiglTJRFGvAEXXnfWyfSz0RcKTdc0SVijutDrGKDjzC7BDwXiykPpSqg3pwIolHdg3emZvghVVSMs5T2veRtSA&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwjjvo-ky5KRAxXhAvsDHSaKBlEQgK4QegYIAQgAEAc&biw=1912&bih=924&dpr=1)) and linking them together to form a web of knowledge. This differs from traditional linear note-taking by focusing on emergent connections between ideas rather than pre-defined hierarchies
